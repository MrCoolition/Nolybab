How the world moves

You author concrete civic moves. Every target, culture, verb, method, and intensity changes the cost and forecast before you commit.

  1. Choose whatMake a law, shape a culture, invent something, or build a new way of living.
  2. Choose whereTouch a pressure, arrival, settlement, region, living artifact, or the Commons in the world or Target lane.
  3. Choose whoName a lead culture and, if you want friction or wider consent, add an ally.
  4. Choose howAct with an immediate verb through witness, prototype, ritual, boundary, reciprocity, remembrance, or play. Set the force and name the result.
  5. CommitRead the explicit resource cost, risk, likely pressure shift, and artifact forecast. Act now, interrupt autonomy, or reroute the pressure elsewhere.
